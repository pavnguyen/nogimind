# Body Position Precision Model

Body Position converts old body-to-body details into an attacker/defender contact model.

## Required Shape

- At least three phases.
- At least four contact cards.
- Every contact shows attacker target to defender target.
- Every contact includes exact instruction, why it works, correction cue, and live cue.

## UI Model

- Attacker Body Map: head, chest, hips, left hand, right hand, left leg, right leg.
- Defender Body Map: head, chest, hips, left arm, right arm, left leg, right leg.
- Contact cards render the exact contact, force direction, pressure level, why, correction, and safety notes.

## Priority Enrichment

RNC, Arm Triangle, Kimura, Heel Hook Safety, Back Control, Bodylock Passing, Knee Cut Passing, Side Control Escape, Mount Escape, Front Headlock Defense, and the modern expansion systems have body-position data. Armbar, Triangle, and Omoplata were promoted into implemented skills with precise body-position systems.
