# Content Style Guide

## Voice

Write like a precise no-gi coach. Keep sentences short, directional, and body-part specific.

## Good

- My right hand pulls the opponent's left wrist across centerline.
- My chest drives diagonally toward the opponent's far hip.
- My choking elbow aligns under the opponent's chin before squeezing.
- Hide the heel before rotating.

## Avoid

- Control the arm.
- Put your foot on the hip.
- Apply pressure.
- Finish the choke.

## Vietnamese

Keep standard BJJ words when they are clearer: underhook, crossface, bodylock, knee line, front headlock, ashi garami, saddle, heel hook, RNC.

Use Vietnamese around the term to make meaning clear:

- Giữ knee line, tức là đầu gối chưa bị kẹt sau hip line.
- Tay phải kéo wrist trái qua centerline.

## French

Keep common grappling terms when useful: underhook, crossface, bodylock, knee line, front headlock, saddle, heel hook.

## Safety

Safety-sensitive content must say:

- apply gradually
- release on tap or distress
- tap early
- do not crank
- do not spin blindly
- clear knee line before rotation
- train under qualified supervision

Do not give medical advice.
