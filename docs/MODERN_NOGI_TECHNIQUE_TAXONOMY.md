# Modern No-Gi Technique Taxonomy

This document organizes modern no-gi techniques into logical systems for the NoGi Mind technical database.

## A. Core Submissions
*Reliable finishers that form the backbone of any submission system.*
- RNC (Rear Naked Choke)
- Armbar (Standard / S-Mount)
- Triangle (Front / Inverted)
- Guillotine (Classic)
- Arm Triangle (Mount / Side)
- Kimura (Classic)
- Omoplata
- D’Arce
- Anaconda
- North-South Choke
- Straight Ankle Lock
- Heel Hook (Inside / Outside)
- Kneebar
- Toe Hold

## B. Modern & Specialized Submissions
*Evolved variations and niche finishers seen in high-level no-gi competition.*
- **Front Headlock Variations**: High-Wrist Guillotine, Low-Wrist Guillotine, High-Elbow Guillotine, Japanese Necktie, Peruvian Necktie.
- **Triangle Variations**: Rear Triangle, Mounted Triangle, Back Triangle Armbar.
- **Advanced Guards / Dynamic**: Gogoplata, Locoplata, Buggy Choke, Reverse Buggy Choke.
- **Modern Armlocks**: Choi Bar, Tarikoplata, Baratoplata.
- **Leg Lock Specialties**: Aoki Lock, Z-Lock, Estima Lock, Shotgun Ankle Lock, Texas Cloverleaf.
- **Pressure Submissions**: Smother / Chest Compression.

## C. Guard Systems
*Modern guard configurations optimized for the no-gi environment.*
- **Seated Guard Systems**: Butterfly, Shin-to-Shin.
- **Supine Guard Systems**: Half Guard, Knee Shield, Dogfight.
- **Open Guard Specialists**: Single Leg X, X Guard, K-Guard, Matrix.
- **Alternative Controls**: Octopus Guard, Clamp Guard, Shoulder Crunch, Overhook Guard, High Guard.
- **Leg Entanglements**: 50/50, False Reap, Reverse De La Riva No-Gi, No-Gi De La Riva.

## D. Passing Systems
*Methodical and explosive ways to navigate the modern guard.*
- **Pressure Passing**: Bodylock Passing, Chest-to-Chest Half Guard Passing, Headquarters, Tripod Passing, Leg Pin Passing, Double Under Stack.
- **Mobility Passing**: Knee Cut, Split Squat Passing, Float Passing, Long-Step, Backstep, Leg Drag, Shin Staple, Torreando No-Gi, Outside Passing.

## E. Rides & Back Takes
*Controlling the opponent's torso and hips to secure the back.*
- **Torso Rides**: Crab Ride, Wrist Ride, Cross-Wrist Ride, Spiral Ride, Claw Ride, Power Half, Leg Ride.
- **Hip Control**: Near Ankle Ride, Far Ankle Ride, Dagestani Handcuff.
- **Transitionary**: Chair Sit Back Take, Turtle Ride, Truck / Twister Hook.

## F. Wrestle-Ups & Scrambles
*Using the ground and underhooks to return to the feet or take top position.*
- **Underhook Systems**: Half Guard Underhook to Dogfight, Dogfight Knee Tap, Dogfight Back Take.
- **Dynamic Returns**: Hip Heist Wrestle-Up, Low Single from Seated Guard, Shin-to-Shin Wrestle-Up.
- **Counters**: Front Headlock Counter Wrestle-Up.
- **Scramble Mechanics**: Sit-Out, Peek-Out, Granby Recovery, Switch, Limp Arm, Whizzer Defense.

## G. Leg Lock Systems
*Comprehensive control and exposure of the lower limbs.*
- **Positioning**: Outside Ashi, Inside Ashi, Cross Ashi, Saddle (Inside Sankaku), 50/50, Backside 50/50.
- **Mechanics**: Knee Line control, Heel Exposure, Leg Pummeling.
- **Finishes**: Ankle Lock, Heel Hook, Aoki Lock, Z-Lock, Toe Hold, Kneebar.

## H. Safety-Critical Techniques
*Techniques requiring special awareness to prevent injury.*
- **Lower Limb**: Heel Hook Safety, False Reap Safety, Reaping Awareness, Knee Line Defense.
- **Spine & Neck**: Neck Crank Recognition, Spine Lock Awareness, Front Headlock Safety.
- **Force / Impact**: Slam Risk Awareness, Kani Basami Safety, Flying Attack Safety.
- **Outcome Awareness**: Compression vs Strangle, Late Tap Danger Signals.
